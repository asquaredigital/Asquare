OnScreen Theater Advertisement

Our Advertising Partner: Annai Associates - Annai Ads

Why advertise in theaters? Here are some reasons:

  • No remote to change the channel: Unlike other forms of advertising, viewers in a theater cannot easily change the channel or skip over ads, which means that the audience is more likely to pay attention to your message.
  • Brand recall: Advertising in theaters can be an effective way to create strong brand recall among viewers.
  • Cheapest medium for on-screen visual and slide ads: Advertising in theaters can be a cost-effective way to reach a large audience with your visual or slide-based ads.
  • Reach: Advertising in a theater can help you reach a larger audience, as cinema has more craze among people compared to other mediums.
  • Opportunities for special offers: Advertising during popular movies and festivals can give your company's offers and new product launches a greater reach.

Why choose Asquare? Here are some reasons:

  • Direct and indirect tie-ups with top theaters: Asquare has direct and indirect tie-ups with the top multiplex theaters and individual theaters across Tamil Nadu, which means that we can help you reach a wide audience.
  • Reasonable and flexible prices: We offer reasonable and flexible prices to our customers based on their specific requirements.
  • Help with video and slide design: We can help our clients design effective video and slide-based ads for the big screen.
  • Prompt and timely delivery: We guarantee prompt and timely delivery of ad content to theaters.
  • Replacement of old ads: We allow our clients to replace old ads with new ones multiple times in our direct-dealing theaters.
  • Proof of ad: We provide proof of ad placement if needed.
  • Responsive customer support: We are available to answer any queries our clients may have.
